STAY ON TRACK WITH ACCURATE LOCATION

A picturesque place surrounded by the majestic Shivalik range of the Himalayas. It is encircled by stunning natural scenery, and it is a popular destination for both Indian and international tourists. The Guest House is situated near the banks of the river Ganges. If you’re looking for a lively and alternative place to stay by the Ganges river, this is the perfect spot. A short walk distance away to yoga ashrams, adventure tour outfits, and riverside banks . You can relax with a cup of coffee and pancakes at terrace with river views, or explore the small shops selling quirky clothing and crafts. Lakshman Jhula and Ram Jhula, the bridges over the Ganges river, are spectacular, and very breathtaking and give a great view and feel of the almighty Ganga flowing so peacefully. It is also a great place to walk and meditate, as well as to enjoy delicious Indian food.
